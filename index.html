FAQ

Why another GURPS character generator?
MyRpgChar will be optimized for different screen sizes. It will be usable not only on PC screens but also on smartphones and tablets.
What's a MyRpgChar for?
MyRpgChar is planed to be a character-generator as well as aid during a GURPS gamming session. It will be web-based service running in the web-browser of most modern tablets and smartphones.
